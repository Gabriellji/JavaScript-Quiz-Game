Welcome to About Us

Our group is made up of Roxanne, Jane, Nadine and Viet. We are a group of web developers from different parts of the world that have come together to build this project. The aim of the project was to highlight our design and coding skills which has been demonstrated through a game. We have developed the characters and a storyline to engage the player. The stylistic aspect of the game is done intentionally to make it more appealing to a similar audience as ourselves. Each member of the group was delegated a role in creating specific aspects of the game which we ultimately combined to demonstrate our group's abilities. We hope you enjoy our game as much as we enjoyed creating it together.
